General Rules

We will be playing 5 rounds of bingo. The details of each round are outlined below.
A round is over when the first person complets a win of at least one of their boards and yells, "BINGO" to notify the group.
In the event that more than one person yells "BINGO" (and actually has BINGO) at the same time, the prize for that round will be split evenly among the winners
You should clear your board(s) and use them again in each round.
- Ex. If you have five boards, you should be playing with five boards in round-1, five boards in round-2, five boards in round-3, five boards in round-4 and five boards in round-5.
We will check to make sure the person that yells BINGO actually has won, so please wait to clear your boards until after we can confirm the winner.

Ali's Pro Tip

If you are playing with printed boards, you can put your board in a ziplock bag and use dry erase markers to mark your board and easily erase after each round. Or you can cover your numbers with pennies. (Ali recommends dimes over pennies)
